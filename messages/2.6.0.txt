Sublime-RGBDS 2.6.0 changelog:

Catching up with new RGBDS features!

 - New `\0` escape character in strings
 - `cpl a`
 - Better handling of incomplete `def`/`redef` lines
 - Fixed some invalid internal syntax, which enables compat with Sublime Text's new regex engine
 - The `.` special symbol name
 - Nested macro definitions weren't properly rejected
